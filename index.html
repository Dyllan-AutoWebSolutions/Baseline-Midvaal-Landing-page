Who We Are

A Family,
Not Just a Church

Baseline Family Church Midvaal is a branch of Baseline Family Church Bassonia — carrying the same DNA of purposeful, grounded faith — but rooted right here in our local community with a heart for the people of Midvaal.

We are a community that takes faith seriously and people personally. Fellowship, love, and belonging are not extras here — they are the foundation of everything we do.

Our mission is simple: spread the Gospel, disciple believers, and build a community where people can experience transformative love in Christ.
